Security

Framework

PFA is built upon Django framework which is famous for its security.

Encryption

All of your data will be encrypted before being transmitted through the network or stored in database
The encryption follows Advanced Encryption Standard(AES) and your private key will not be stored in any database
Interface

Simple

All interfaces are simple, pragmatic.
The system goal is well focused so the functionality is clear to all

Intuitive

As long as you can read in English
Professional

Visualization

Pyecharts visualization library is adopted to generate informative and interactive graphs for you to explore your own data
Future

Login

Of course, you will be able to login and enjoy all the funs

Why not get the source on github and set up your own application on your own server? That will greatly improve your security level

More visualization

You will see more visualization available, you can even personalize the visualization methods and explore by yourself

Better connection with your real life

Don't need input by yourself, PFA will connect with your bank cards, online payment apps directly! :-)

Techs behind


The motivation of building this application comes from my family. I can't wait helping my parents recording their daily financial activities.

Long Long ago, we record our daily transaction on paper. It is hard to do statistic and that hurt our motivation

With PFA, life become easier. It helps you better plan your future expenditure and evaluate your life quality

Hope that you can enjoy and learn the importance of money management
